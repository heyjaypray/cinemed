ACS Video Library

The American College of Surgeon's Online Video Library is the easiest, most innovative, and cost-efficient way to view the entire Collection of ACS-selected narrated surgical procedures. More than 2,000 videos are available in a wide selection of body system and procedure type, and new videos are added each year. Residency programs take note! This video library is an excellent teaching resource for residents, as each video is narrated and edited to the particulars of the procedure. Enhance your Grand Rounds by assigning videos for residents to watch beforehand and come prepared to discuss. The library contains the following categories of videos: Bariatric Surgery, Cardiothoracic Surgery, Challenging and Unusual Problems in Surgery, Colon and Rectal Surgery, Endocrine Surgery, General Surgery, Hepatobiliary Surgery, Hernia Surgery, Highlights from International Meetings, Neurological Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Orthopaedic Surgery, Otolaryngology (Head and Neck Surgery), Pancreas Surgery, Pediatric Surgery, Surgical History, Trauma Surgery, Urologic Surgery, and Vascular Surgery.
